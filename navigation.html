New game with no boundary


Instructions

* Click the new game to start the game. The game will auto start with out any keypress

* Use up,right,left and down button to move the snake to a specified postion.

* When the snake eats food the length of the snake increases by one and gets a score of 5.

* When the snake eats 4 foods consecutively the bonus starts , if the snake eats the bonus food within the predefined time
    the player will get 50 bonus points.

* When the snake touches its body or the boundary of the left,right,up or down region the snake dies.
