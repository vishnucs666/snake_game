SNAKE GAME

New Game

Instructions


* To start the game press right,up or down key
* When the snake eats food the length of the snake increases by one and gets a score of 5
* When the snake eats 4 foods consecutively the bonus starts , if the snake eats the bonus food within the predefined time the player will get 50 bonus points
* When the snake touches its body or the boundary of the left,right,up or down region the snake dies
